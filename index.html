Frequently Asked Questions

How is EMI calculated?
EMI is calculated using the reducing balance method based on loan amount, interest rate and tenure.
What is SIP?
SIP stands for Systematic Investment Plan. It allows investors to invest a fixed amount regularly.
How many square feet are in 1 Gaj?
